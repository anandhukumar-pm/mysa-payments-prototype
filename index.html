Interactive prototype for the payments approval and authorisation flow. Select a role below to explore the experience.

Creates payments and tracks them through the approval workflow. Has no approval or authorisation actions on their own payments.

Finance Controller
FC — Payment Creator + Approver
Creates their own payments and also approves payments created by other FCs. Sees both In Queue (for approval) and Pending on Others (their own payments).
In Queue Approve / Reject Pending on Others
Admin
Admin — Approver + Authoriser
Approves and authorises payments. In Queue shows both pending approvals and payments ready to authorise. Authorisation requires transaction password and OTP verification.
